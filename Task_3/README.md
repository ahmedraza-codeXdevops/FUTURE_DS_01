# 📈 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes marketing campaign performance and customer conversion behavior. It helps track the customer journey through the marketing funnel, measure conversion rates, and identify the factors influencing successful conversions.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer conversion rates
- Track marketing funnel performance
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness
- Identify high-performing customer segments
- Generate actionable business insights

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Total Customers
- Converted Customers
- Conversion Rate
- Customer Conversion Funnel
- Converted Customers by Education
- Converted Customers by Job
- Converted Customers by Contact Method
- Monthly Campaign Performance
- Interactive Filters (Job & Education)

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- Bank Marketing Dataset
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization

## 📂 Dataset

**Dataset:** Bank Marketing Dataset

## 📈 Key Insights

- Overall conversion rate is approximately **10%**.
- The marketing funnel highlights the number of customers converted from the total customer base.
- Conversion performance varies across different job categories.
- Education level influences customer conversion.
- Cellular contact method generates the highest conversions.
- Monthly analysis helps identify the most effective campaign periods.
